Veggie Burrito

Description

The ultimate Vegetarian Burrito recipe featuring cilantro-lime rice, chipotle-spiced black beans, sautéed peppers and onions, and a zesty avocado cream sauce. These veggie burritos are freezer-friendly and perfect for weekday meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black beans
  • Fajita veggies
  • Spices
  • Adobo Sauce
  • Avocado
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Tortillas

Steps

  • Blend avocado cream sauce.

    To a food processor add:

    • Avocade
    • Cilantro
    • Jalapeno
    • Garlic
    • Lime Juice
    • Sour cream
  • Prepare cilantro-lime rice.

    In a small saucepan combine:

    • 2 cups of water
    • 1/2 tsp. salt
    • Bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium-low
    • Cover and gently simmer for 18 minutes
    • Remove heat and stir in:
      • 3 tsp. chopped cilantro
      • 1/2 tsp lime zest
      • 1 tsp fresh lime juice
  • Saute fajita veggies
    • Heat 2 tsp oil in a large skillet over medium high
    • Once hot, add:
      • Bell peppers
      • Poblano pepper
      • Red onion
    • Cook 7 to 9 minutes stirring occasionally
    • Stir in 1 tsp taco seasoning and remove from heat
  • Prepare the Chipotle black beans
    • Drain and rinse the beans
    • Shake off excess water and transfer to a bowl
    • Stir in:
      • Ground cumin
      • A pinch of salt
    • Cover with a damp towel and microwave for 2 minutes
    • Wrap your burrito and Enjoy!
